Floating\organisms that live in water and lack the ability to move efficiently are called plankton.

Drifting \ Plankton is mainly driven by the flow of the movement, the increasing pollution of the sea, the need to find a remote habitat.

Space

日本福岛核电站：N37°25'20.60" E141°1'57.90"
福岛核电站废水排放趋势图



2021年4月，日本将百万吨核废水排入大海。日本太平洋海域将受到严重影响，特别是福岛县周边局部水域。一家来自德国的海洋科学研究机构的计算结果显示，从排放之日起，57天内放射性物质就将扩散至太平洋大半区域，3年后美国和加拿大就将遭到核污染影响。我们将装置放置在距离福岛沿岸100KM的蓝色污染海域中，拯救浮游生物的同时检测水质，收集海洋数据。
In April 2021, Japan releases a million tons of nuclear waste water into the sea. Japan's Pacific waters will be severely affected, especially in the waters around Fukushima prefecture. A German Marine science research institute calculated that within 57 days from the date of discharge, radioactive material would have spread over most of the Pacific Ocean, and three years later the United States and Canada would have been affected by nuclear contamination. We put the device in the blue polluted ocean 100KM off the coast of Fukushima to rescue plankton and monitor the water quality and collect oceanic data.
